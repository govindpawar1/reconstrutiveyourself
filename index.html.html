# Activity Progress How to Do It
1 Practice Mindfulness
Start with 5 minutes of deep breathing exercises daily. Gradually increase to 10-15 minutes.
2 Exercise Regularly
Create a weekly plan with 3-5 sessions of cardio, strength training, or yoga.
3 Read Personal Growth Books
Start with 15 minutes of daily reading. Choose books like *Atomic Habits* or *The Power of Now*.
4 Learn a New Skill
Pick a skill like coding, cooking, or playing an instrument. Dedicate 30 minutes daily to practice.
5 Write a Daily Journal
Spend 10 minutes each evening reflecting on your day and setting goals for tomorrow.
